Biryani

Biryani is a fragrant, flavorful dish of layered rice and meat, seafood, eggs, or vegetables, spiced with a unique blend of regional herbs and spices, and cooked slowly using the dum-style of slow steaming or pressure cooking to allow the flavors to meld.

Ingredients

  • rice
  • leaves
  • spices
  • curd
  • chicken

Process

  1. Marinate your choice of meat or vegetables in yogurt and a blend of biryani spices.
  2. Par-boil long-grain basmati rice with whole spices and fry sliced onions until golden.
  3. Cook the marinated meat separately until it's about 80% done.
  4. Layer the cooked meat and rice in a heavy-bottomed pot, sprinkling fried onions and herbs between layers.
  5. Seal the pot and cook on low heat (dum) for 20-25 minutes to steam everything together.
