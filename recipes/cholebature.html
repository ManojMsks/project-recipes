Chole Bhature

Chole Bhature is a popular North Indian dish consisting of a spicy chickpea curry (Chole) served with a fluffy, deep-fried leavened bread called Bhature. It's often enjoyed as a hearty breakfast or lunch.

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Onions and Tomatoes
  • All-purpose flour (Maida)
  • Yogurt
  • Spices (Coriander, Cumin)

Process

  1. Soak chickpeas overnight and boil them until tender with tea bags for color.
  2. Create a masala by saut√©ing onions, ginger, garlic, and tomatoes with powdered spices.
  3. Combine the boiled chickpeas with the masala and simmer to create the Chole curry.
  4. Prepare a soft dough for the Bhature using flour, yogurt, and a leavening agent, then let it rest.
  5. Roll out portions of the dough into discs and deep-fry them in hot oil until they puff up and turn golden brown.
