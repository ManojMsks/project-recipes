Paneer Tikka

Paneer Tikka is a popular vegetarian appetizer where cubes of paneer (Indian cheese) are marinated in a spiced yogurt mixture and then grilled or baked until golden. It's often served with mint chutney and onion rings.

Ingredients

  • Paneer (Indian Cheese)
  • Yogurt (Curd)
  • Bell Peppers (Capsicum)
  • Onion
  • Tandoori Masala

Process

  1. Chop paneer, bell peppers, and onions into bite-sized cubes.
  2. Prepare a thick marinade by mixing yogurt with ginger-garlic paste, tandoori masala, and other spices.
  3. Gently coat the paneer and vegetable cubes in the marinade and let them rest for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Thread the marinated paneer and vegetables onto skewers, alternating between them.
  5. Grill, bake in an oven, or pan-fry the skewers until the paneer is lightly browned and charred at the edges.
