Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken, or Murgh Makhani, is a classic Indian dish where marinated chicken is first grilled and then served in a rich, creamy tomato-based gravy. It's known for its mild flavor and velvety texture.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Tomato Puree
  • Cream
  • Butter
  • Spices (Garam Masala, Turmeric)

Process

  1. Marinate chicken pieces in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices for at least an hour.
  2. Grill or pan-fry the chicken until it's cooked through and slightly charred.
  3. Prepare the gravy by cooking tomato puree with butter, spices, and a bit of sugar.
  4. Simmer the gravy until it thickens, then stir in fresh cream for richness.
  5. Add the cooked chicken to the gravy and let it simmer for a few minutes to absorb the flavors before serving.
